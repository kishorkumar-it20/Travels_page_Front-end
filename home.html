It does not operate any bus or offer the service of transportation to the User. redBus also does not act as an agent of any bus operator in the process of providing the above-mentioned technology platform services. The bus ticket booking voucher which redBus issues to a User is solely based on the information provided or updated by the bus operator regarding the seat availability. The amenities, services, routes, fares, schedule, bus type, seat availability and any other details pertaining to the bus service are provided by the respective bus operator and redBus has no control over such information provided by the bus operator.
